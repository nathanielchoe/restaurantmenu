About US

Welcome to Ivy City's very own US seafood. We are the brother carry-out to the well-known, sister establishment Tony's Breakfast on H Street, NE. Have you heard of US? If not check out the link below. It has been our pleasure to serve our local community for over two decades and we appreciate your continued support!

Breakfast

Breakfast sandwiches come with a fried egg and on a choice of white or wheat toast. To upgrade to croissant, bagel, English muffin, or to add cheese is an additional charge. Breakfast boxes come with two scrambled eggs, your choice of a breakfast side, and white or wheat toast. Cheese is additional. Breakfast omelets include sauted spinach, onions, tomatoes, green peppers, mushrooms, and cheese. Add a side of homefries or grits for just $1.
